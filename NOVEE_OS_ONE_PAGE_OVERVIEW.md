# NOVEE OS — One Page Overview

> Prototype / pilot-ready system. Live venue data not connected unless configured.

---

## What It Is

NOVEE OS is a guest intelligence platform for premium hospitality venues. It connects the guest experience — what they taste, learn, and choose — to the operational layer of the venue, giving staff, managers, and owners a clear picture of what's working and why.

---

## The Three Layers

**Guest Layer — The Experience**  
Guests engage with a premium, mentor-guided craft experience on a tablet or kiosk. SmokeCraft guides them through cigar education — origins, flavor profiling, mentor selection, and pairing. A Passport stamp records every session. A leaderboard creates return visit behavior.

**Staff Layer — The Intelligence**  
POS 3 shows staff the guest's session in real time — what they explored, what their flavor profile is, and what to recommend. No extensive product training required. The right context at the right moment.

**Manager Layer — The Command**  
E.A.T. Command gives managers a live view of Environment (table activity, session flow), Asset (which products and mentors are driving engagement), and Transaction (does session completion correlate to orders?).

## What It Runs On

Any modern iPad or Android tablet. No app to download — installs as a web app in under 2 minutes. Works on standard venue Wi-Fi. Offline fallback built in.

---

## What It Requires

- One tablet (10th gen iPad or equivalent)
- Stable Wi-Fi on premises
- 30 minutes with staff
- One session with your manager
- A compliance review for tobacco and/or alcohol regulations

---

## What It Does Not Do

- It does not process payments
- It does not age-verify guests
- It does not replace your POS system
- It does not require guests to create an account
- It does not contain live data unless live integrations are configured
- It does not make revenue claims
