# NOVEE OS — Guest Test Script
## Phase 12: Real-World Venue Testing

---

## For the Observer: Setup Line

When the participant approaches the device, say exactly this:

> **"Walk up to the screen and begin the SmokeCraft experience."**

Nothing more. Do not point. Do not demonstrate.

If they ask what to do:

> **"Just do whatever feels natural."**

If they are stuck for more than 90 seconds:

> **"You can tap anything on the screen to explore."**

---

## What the Guest Should Be Able to Do Without Help

1. Find the SmokeCraft module from the home screen
2. Start the experience
3. Choose a mentor
4. Listen to voice guidance (or mute it)
5. Navigate through the experience steps
6. Complete the session
7. See their Passport stamp
8. Find the leaderboard
9. Return home

---

## Hints (Only If Genuinely Stuck — 90+ Seconds)

| Stuck On | Hint |
|---|---|
| How to start | "You can tap anywhere on the screen." |
| Mentor selection | "There are different options — you can choose one." |
| How to mute | "There may be audio controls on the screen." |
| How to go back | "Look for a button to go to the previous screen or home." |

Do not give more than one hint per session.

---

## After the Session

Thank the participant:

> **"Thank you — that was really helpful."**

Do not ask for opinions during the session. If they volunteer feedback, note it as an observer note.

If you debrief afterward, useful questions are:
- "Was anything confusing?"
- "What was your favourite part?"
- "Would you use this at a venue?"
- "What did you expect to happen when you tapped [X]?"
