# Venue Owner Demo Script — NOVEE OS

> Phase 13 · Founder / Investor / Pilot Package  
> Route: `/venue-demo` — Manager+ access  
> Tone: clear, premium, non-technical

---

## Setup

- Run this script in a lounge or meeting room setting
- Have a tablet in landscape orientation on a stand
- Log in as manager before the venue owner arrives
- Navigate to `/venue-demo` for the structured walkthrough overview
- Have `/crafthub` ready to demonstrate the guest journey live

---

## Opening (30 seconds)

> "I want to show you something that changes how guests experience your venue — and how your staff and managers understand what's happening on the floor. It takes about 10 minutes. May I?"

Set the tablet in front of them. Let them look at the idle screen for a moment.

> "This is what a guest sees when they approach the station."

---

## Part 1: Why Guests Care (2 minutes)

Tap to start. Navigate into SmokeCraft.

> "A guest walks over and taps the screen. They choose a mentor — someone whose approach matches their taste — and begin a guided experience. It's not a quiz. It's not a menu. It's a real education."

Let the mentor voice play for 30 seconds.

> "The voice is AI-generated, but it's personalised to each mentor's character. In a loud environment, guests can read along instead — we built that in from day one."

Navigate to the Passport stamp at the end of a session.

> "At the end, they earn a stamp on their Passport. The leaderboard shows the most experienced guests at your venue. Guests come back to earn more stamps, move up the rankings, and try new mentors. Not because of discounts — because they actually care about the experience."

---

## Part 2: Why Staff Care (2 minutes)

Navigate to `/pos`.

> "While a guest is completing their session, your staff can see it in real time. This is POS 3 — your staff-facing companion."

Point to the active order feed and table status.

> "When the session finishes, a recommendation preview appears — based on exactly what that guest just learned. Your staff don't need to know every product in your humidor. The system tells them what to suggest."

> "The conversation changes from 'Can I help you?' to 'You just explored the Connecticut Shade — we have three in-stock that match your profile perfectly.'"

---

## Part 3: Why Managers Care (2 minutes)

Navigate to `/eat`.

> "This is E.A.T. Command — your management layer. Environment, Asset, Transaction."

Point to the Environment feed.

> "Environment shows table occupancy and session activity across the venue in real time."

Point to the Asset feed.

> "Asset shows which products, mentors, and blends are actually driving engagement tonight — not what sold last month, but what's working right now."

Point to the Transaction panel.

> "Transaction connects the dots — when a guest completes a SmokeCraft session and then places an order, that correlation appears here. You can see whether the experience is driving revenue."

> "The question every manager asks is: 'What's actually working on my floor tonight?' This answers it."

---

## Part 4: The Install (1 minute)

Navigate to `/kiosk-setup`.

> "This installs on any iPad as a web app — no app store, no IT department. Your staff adds it to the Home Screen in under 2 minutes. It runs in kiosk mode so guests can't accidentally navigate away. If your Wi-Fi drops, the guest experience continues — it syncs when connectivity comes back."

---

## Part 5: What a Pilot Looks Like (1 minute)

Navigate to `/system-overview` or `/pilot-onboarding`.

> "A pilot starts with one tablet. We walk your staff through POS 3 in 30 minutes. We run a structured test session with a few guests before you go live — we have a readiness score that tells us exactly when you're ready."

> "No payment is collected today. No long contract. One tablet, your venue, real guest data."

---

## Handling Questions

### "What does it cost?"
> "Pricing for pilot partners is set on a case-by-case basis — I'd like to understand your venue's needs first. What I can tell you is that the first tier starts with a single tablet and very low commitment."

### "Do guests need to sign up?"
> "No sign-up, no app download, no personal data required. In the commercial version, guests can optionally carry their Passport between visits — but it's entirely their choice."

### "Does it replace our POS?"
> "No — POS 3 works alongside your existing system. It adds guest context to what you're already doing. It doesn't touch payments or replace anything."

### "What about tobacco laws / compliance?"
> "NOVEE OS provides tasting guidance and education content only. No product is sold through the system. Your staff controls all actual service decisions. We include a compliance review as part of the pilot setup."

### "How long does it take to set up?"
> "For a single-tablet pilot — one day. We configure the device, walk your staff through POS 3, and run a test session. You could be guest-facing the next morning."

> "What I'd like to do is register your venue as a pilot partner and schedule a setup session. There's no cost today — just a 30-minute call with your manager to confirm the setup requirements."

Navigate to `/pilot-onboarding`.

> "This is the pilot registration form. We can fill it out together right now, or I can send it to you to complete."
