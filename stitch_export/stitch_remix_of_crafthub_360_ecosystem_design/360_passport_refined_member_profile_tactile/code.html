Julian Thorne is a distinguished curator of rare experiences, specializing in the intersection of traditional craftsmanship and modern luxury. With over two decades in the high-stakes world of auction houses and private estates, he brings an unparalleled eye for the 'Authentic Soul' of an object.

His tenure at The 360 Passport has been defined by his commitment to preserving artisanal methods that are slowly fading. From the rolling hills of Scottish distilleries to the hidden ateliers of Florence, Julian bridges the gap between the maker and the connoisseur.
