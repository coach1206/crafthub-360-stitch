## The Problem

Premium hospitality venues — cigar lounges, craft beverage bars, private clubs, wine rooms — invest heavily in product quality and ambiance. But they capture almost nothing actionable about the guests who walk through their doors.

The result:

- Staff cannot personalise recommendations at scale without years of product expertise
- Guests leave with a good experience but no ongoing relationship with the venue
- Managers make floor decisions based on intuition, not data
- POS systems record what was purchased — but nothing about what preceded the purchase

This is not a technology gap. Venues already have tablets. They already have Wi-Fi. The gap is the layer that connects the guest's experience to the venue's intelligence.

---

## The Solution

NOVEE OS is a guest intelligence platform that sits between the guest experience and the venue's operational layer.

Three things happen simultaneously:

1. **Guests engage** — A premium, mentor-guided craft experience on a tablet or kiosk. They choose a mentor, learn about origins, flavor, and pairing, and complete a session that generates a behavioral profile.

2. **Staff get context** — POS 3 shows staff what a guest just experienced, what their flavor profile is, and what to recommend. No product training required.

3. **Managers get intelligence** — E.A.T. Command shows which products and mentors are driving engagement, which tables are active, and whether session completions correlate to transactions.

The 360 Passport is the data layer — stamps, streaks, and leaderboard rankings that give guests a reason to return without discounts or generic loyalty schemes.

## Founder Vision

The goal is not to build another loyalty app. The goal is to make every premium hospitality venue as intelligent as the best-run venue in the world — where every guest interaction compounds into a relationship, and every manager decision is informed by what's actually happening on the floor.

NOVEE OS is the connective tissue that makes this possible. It starts with cigars because the cigar lounge is the clearest example of a premium, experience-led venue that has almost no technology serving the relationship between the guest and the product. But the platform is the same across every craft category.
